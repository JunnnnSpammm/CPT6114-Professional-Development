# Assignment Part 1: User Manual

## 1. Overview of the Product
This ecosystem integrates a Smart Pencil with a 50,000 mAh case that serves as both a high-capacity power bank and an interactive touchscreen. The system bridges physical and digital workflows by offering direct-on-case writing, text scanning, audio transcription, and lost-device tracking in a single mobile unit.

## 4. Operation Guidelines

This section provides and explains step-by-step instructions on how to set up/operate with the Smart Pencil and Pencil Case effectively.

### 4.1 Charging and Power Management

**Wireless Charging:**
1. Place the pencil above the screen on the pencil case and make sure it sticks to the magnet. The pencil case will then charge the pencil automatically upon contact.

**Wired Charging:**
1. Connect the Type-C cable to the Pencil Case.
2. Connect the Type-C cable to the Pencil.

### 4.2 Adaptive Pencil’s Touch Screen Interaction

1. Make sure the pencil has enough battery to operate (at least 20 percent of battery level before use).
2. Turn on the pencil case interface.
3. Control the interface using the pencil touch screen sensor or finger.

### 4.3 Pencil Tracking and Recovery
1. To find a misplaced pencil, navigate to the find my pencil app.
2. If the pencil has enough battery, and is within 50 m, press “Play Sound” to tell the misplaced pencil to play sounds and vibrate to assist in locating it.
3. Otherwise, check the map and for the pencil’s last known location.

## 5. Troubleshooting Guide

This section explains step-by-step to resolve related issues.

### 5.1 Charging Issues
1. Make sure the pencil is placed correctly in place to ensure the coils are aligned
2. Try using a different USB-C cable and power adapter to check what has caused the problem.
3. Try restarting it by holding down the power button.

### 5.3 Pencil Tracking Issues

"Find My Pencil" feature will only work with the conditions below:

-	The pencil is powered on and within a 50 m range.
-	The pencil is still paired with the pencil case.
-	The pencil’s battery level is sufficient.

If it is certain that all the conditions are satisfied, but the feature is not working:

1. Head to Settings > Location Services and turn it off and on.
2. Restart the pencil case.
3. If issues persist, please contact customer service immediately for assistance.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **Can I charge other devices?** Yes. The case features an auxiliary USB-C port, allowing it to function as a universal power bank for smartphones and tablets.
2. **Is the touch screen durable?** Yes. The interface is built with reinforced, scratch-resistant glass designed to withstand the pressure and friction of backpack storage.
3. **What is the tracking range?** The system uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), which maintains a stable connection within a 10 to 15-meter radius.
4. **Is the internet required for translation usage?** Basic scanning and transcription work offline for privacy, while advanced translation requires a Wi-Fi or data connection.
