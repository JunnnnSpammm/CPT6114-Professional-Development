2. How do you think fake news impacts society? Do you believe fake news affects people’s opinions and behaviours? If so, how?

Fake news affects people’s opinions and behaviours. We naturally favour information that supports what we already believe. Fake news is 
designed to spark anger, fear, or outrage. The more we hear a lie, the more likely we are to believe it is true.

The latest advances of technology is AI. It can benefit us by handling repetitive digital tasks without human supervision, freeing up time

face disruption.

4. What are the professional certifications that are typically obtained in the IT industry? 

Vendor certification, relevant for narrowly defined roles or certain aspects of broader role. Require passing a written exam, or in some 
cases, a hands-on lab to demonstrate skills and knowledge. Industry association certification, a formal credential from a professional 
organization recognizing an individual's proven expertise, skills, or knowledge in a specific field. Professional Certification, MBOT is a 
professional body that gives Professional Recognition to Technologists and Technicians in related technology and technical fields.
